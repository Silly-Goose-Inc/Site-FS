About

FishStix is an independent art and vinyl studio focused on original design, small-batch sticker production, and hands-on fabrication. Everything is designed, cut, and assembled with intention—no mass churn, no filler.

What We Make

  • Original art stickers (vinyl, weather-resistant)
  • Limited-run designs and drops
  • Custom vinyl projects
  • Experimental and collaborative pieces

Process

Design begins on paper or screen, moves through iteration, and ends with physical fabrication. Constraints are embraced. Materials matter.

Small runs allow control, learning, and variation. Imperfection is not a defect—it’s evidence of a hand.
